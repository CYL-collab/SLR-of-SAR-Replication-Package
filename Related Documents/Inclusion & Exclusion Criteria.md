### Inclusion

```
IC1: The paper explicitly discusses software aging and/or rejuvenation as a main research topic.

IC2: The paper provides sufficient context to understand the SAR-related contribution.

IC3: If the paper proposes a new method or technique related to SAR, it must include validation or evaluation to demonstrate its effectiveness.

IC4: The study presents original empirical evidence, analytical results, or methodological contributionsï¼Œrather than secondary reviews.

```

### Exclusion

```
EC1: The paper is not written in English.

EC2: The paper was not published in a peer-reviewed journal, conference, or workshop proceedings.

EC3: The paper is published before 2020, but its citation count is smaller than 1.

EC4: If multiple papers from the same research group report overlapping results, only the most complete or extended version is included.

EC5: The paper focuses on software rejuvenation but does not explicitly indicate that its purpose is to mitigate or address software aging.
```
